Dania Ramirez Wiki/Biography

She completed her acting education in New York City under Flo Greenberg. After that, when she was 16 years old she enrolled herself in Montclair State University. She was an amazing volleyball player there.

She was spotted by a modelling scout agency when she was working at a convenience store. She was only 15 years old at that time.
