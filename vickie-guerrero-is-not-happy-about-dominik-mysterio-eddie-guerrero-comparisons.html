Vickie Guerrero Is Not Happy About Dominik Mysterio &amp; Eddie Guerrero Comparisons

August 09, 2024
blog

Eddie Guerrero’s legacy will live on forever, and many fans have labeled other wrestlers as “the next Latino Heat.” Dominik Mysterio’s new gimmick drew comparisons between Rey Mysterio’s son and Eddie Guerrero. It turns out that Vickie Guerrero isn’t a fan of that kind of talk.

An Eddie Guerrero fan page posted a side-by-side graphic of Dominik Mysterio and Eddie Guerrero. The caption said that it was a “like father/like son” kind of situation. The comments seemed to agree, Shaul Guerrero re-posted it and said, “this has got to stop, it’s bullsh*t.”

Vickie Guerrero replied to one fan saying that Dominik Mysterio and Eddie Guerrero look alike, and she didn’t seem happy. After saying “no he’s not” to one fan, she elaborated a bit more further down the comments section.

I’m tired of hearing Dominik compared to Eddie. He will never be Eddie in any way shape or form. It’s plain disrespectful.

Shaul Guerrero, Eddie Guerrero and Vickie Guerrero’s daughter, also gave her two cents. She said, “it’s seriously stupid.” It turns out that the Guerrero family isn’t a fan of this new Dominik Mysterio gimmick.

Dominik Mysterio might not have made things much better for himself in this situation. He recently said that he wishes Eddie Guerrero had won custody of him in that classic angle with Rey Mysterio.

We’ll have to see what is next for Dominik Mysterio. His new character is doing wonders for his career, but that doesn’t mean everyone is a fan of his current WWE character.
