AEW Collision Spoilers For 11/11 (Taped On 11/10)

June 09, 2024
blog

On November 10, All Elite Wrestling taped matches for the November 11 episode of AEW Collision after AEW Rampage from the Oakland Arena in Oakland, California.

The spoilers, courtesy of PWInsider, are as follows:

  • Andrade El Idolo defeated Daniel Garcia; CJ Perry was at ringside.
  • Nick Wayne defeated Dalton Castle.
  • Adam Copeland, Sting & Darby Allin defeated Lance Archer & The Righteous (Dutch & Vincent)
  • RUSH & Dralistico defeates The WorkHorsemen (Anthony Henry & JD Drake)
  • Roderick Strong defeated Darius Martin; Strong was brought to the ring in a wheelchair and, he left in one.
  • Julia Hart defeated Willow Nightingale.
  • Powerhouse Hobbs defeated an unannounced opponent. 
  • After the match, Don Callis cut a promo, saying that he’s buried giants before and he’s happy to have Hobbs bury Paul Wight. They teased a fight between Hobbs and Wight, but they were kept separate.

WrestleZone will have coverage of AEW Collision as it airs on Saturday.
