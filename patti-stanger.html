The Millionaire Matchmaker was a reality series where the show host, Patti Stanger, matched single wealthy people with their compatible dates. Besides hosting, Stanger served as the producer for the show. Since 2019, she has been the executive producer of Matchmaker Mysteries at Hallmark Channel.

The 59-year-old Patti Stanger is an American businesswoman and reality television personality. She is best known for creating The Millionaire Matchmaker, which premiered for eight seasons on Bravo TV. Besides, she is the creator and CEO of a professional matchmaking service for millionaires, Millionaire's Club International, Inc.

Patti Stanger has never been Married

While Stanger earns a hefty sum of money through matchmaking, she can't seem to find a perfect match for herself. Yes, she has never been married to date. However, the love guru was once nearly married to her ex-fiance.

In 2003, Stanger began dating a real-estate executive, Andy Friedman. They met through another matchmaker. In July 2009, Lalate.com revealed that Andy proposed on the occasion of Patti's birthday. However, they disagreed on the wedding date.

The season three of The Millionaire Matchmaker showed Stanger and Andy engaged in wedding planning activities. Further, Patti openly spoke about her wedding details.

Stanger wants to be a Mom

In August 2010, Patti announced via Twitter that her relationship with Friedman came to an end. She stated: "I just ended my relationship with Andy. It hit me really hard that I want kids in my life. You have to agree on the non-negotiables."

Further, Stanger said that their relationship lacked passion since they were “best friends.” Well, it appears that she hasn't found her potential partner, and maybe one day she might start a family.

Stanger's Another Failed Engagement

Family Background

Patricia "Patti" Stanger was born on May 31, 1961, in Short Hills, New Jersey, the United States. Her adoptive parents, Ira and Rhoda Stanger, raised her with the Jewish faith. Patti is a third-generation matchmaker as her mother and grandmother were also a matchmaker.

In 1979, Patti graduated from Millburn High School. In 1983, she graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts from the University of Miami.
